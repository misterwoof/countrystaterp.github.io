Rules

  • Respect others at all times. Racism, hate speech, rape, and sexual harassment are not allowed.
  • Do not grief others. Griefing is the act of intentionally angering or irritating another player with malicious intent. Example: Consistently stalking a player to force RP.
  • No PD baiting. Value for your life/freedom and strive to not get caught in a crime. Jail/ Fines are not to be treated lightly.
  • Do not glitch any elements of the game or use exploits.
  • A player can not kill another player unless:

    • 1: One medic is ingame.
    2: One police is ingame.
    3: Without a reason.
    4: Without RP.

    Failure to follow these rules could result in any of the following, warning, fine, timeout, jail, kick or ban.

  • You are only allowed to rob people for the max of $1,500. You can not force people to take any money out of the bank/ATM's and you can't demand their pin number.
  • Negotiations for a kidnapped person start at a maximum of 50k, no more than that amount can be demanded
  • The police dept can call a time out of robberies. This rule MUST be followed by all.
